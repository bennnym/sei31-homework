Lambda: The Ultimate Declarative

A paper of the utmost historical importance. Many compiler techniques that are in use in languages like JavaScript were first presented here. This paper brings a different perspective to functions. It shows that they can be seen as a naming operation within an environment. The paper takes this expression to logical conclusions, which lead to new compiler techniques.
